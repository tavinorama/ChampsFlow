This is a short, standalone privacy notice for the organicposts.ai landing page waitlist form. It supplements the full Privacy Policy for the specific and limited context of waitlist data collection.

---

## What we collect

When you join the waitlist, we collect:
- Your **email address** (required)
- Your **name** (optional, if provided)

We do not collect payment information, social media credentials, or any other personal data at this stage.

---

## Why we collect it and our lawful basis

| Purpose | Lawful basis (EU) | US basis |
|---|---|---|
| Notify you when Organic Posts launches | Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) — you submitted your email to be notified; our interest in communicating the launch is balanced and expected | Service expectation |
| Send pre-launch marketing emails (tips, updates, product news) | Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — only if you check the optional marketing opt-in box on the form | Opt-in consent |

The marketing opt-in checkbox on the waitlist form is:
- Unchecked by default
- Entirely optional — not joining marketing communications does not affect your place on the waitlist
- Clearly labeled (example: "Send me occasional updates and tips before launch")

---

## How long we keep it

| Data | Retention |
|---|---|
| Email + name (waitlist notification) | Until the Organic Posts service launches, plus 30 days, then deleted unless you have created an account |
| Email (marketing consent) | Until you unsubscribe, or until the service launches + 30 days, whichever comes first — unless you convert to an active user account |
| Marketing consent record | 3 years from date of consent (legal obligation) |

---

## Who sees it

Your email and name are stored in our database (Supabase) and may be used to send transactional or marketing emails via Resend (our email delivery provider). Both Supabase and Resend are sub-processors under our standard data processing agreements.

We do not sell, rent, or share your waitlist data with any third party for their own marketing purposes.

---

## Your rights

You can opt out of marketing emails at any time via the unsubscribe link in every email.

To request deletion of your waitlist data (erasure right), email privacy@organicposts.ai with the subject "Waitlist deletion request" or use the link in any waitlist email you receive.

EU/EEA residents have full GDPR rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection). California residents have CCPA rights. Response within 30 days (EU) or 45 days (CCPA).
