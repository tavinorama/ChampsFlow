## TL;DR

The product is **TrustIndex AI** — an *AI Search Trust Intelligence* SaaS for SMBs.
**OrganicPosts** is demoted to a consultancy sub-brand: *OrganicPosts by TrustIndex AI*.

- **trustindexai.com** = the platform (audit, score, benchmark, plan, monitor). The product. Recurring SaaS.
- **organicposts.ai** = the consultancy (done-with/for-you GEO content execution). The high-margin service upsell.
- Mother phrase: **TrustIndex AI finds the gaps. OrganicPosts helps publish the fix.**
- Primary tagline: **Know if AI trusts your brand.**
- Category to own: **AI Search Trust Intelligence** (NOT "review widgets", NOT "SEO tool").
- Hero metric / owned IP: **TrustIndex Score.**
- Free wedge: **AI Visibility Audit** (lead-gen).

The previously-gated GEO platform spec maps ~1:1 onto TrustIndex AI modules, so the
pivot is mostly renaming + the platform/consultancy split, not a re-architecture.

## Positioning vs Google's 2026 AI-search moves (added 2026-06-10)

**Context**: Google I/O 2026 confirmed AI Mode at 1B+ users and AI Overviews in 25%+ of searches; the June 5 Search Central docs officially recognized GEO/AEO; Google launched a Search Console "AI performance" report (June 3, Google-only, own-site-only, no clicks, no competitors); and Google published 3 questions for clients to vet GEO vendors.

**Our stance — "Google-aligned, multi-engine, radically transparent":**
1. **Complement, not compete, with Search Console.** Google SC tells you that you appeared in Google's AI features. We tell you *why, against whom, across every major engine (ChatGPT/Claude/Perplexity/Gemini + Google), with sentiment and a fix plan.* Lead the comparison, not a denial.
2. **Weaponize Google's vendor-audit checklist.** We pass all three questions: we cite developers.google.com; we align with the official guide (we do NOT score llms.txt or "AI schema"); and our measured-vs-baseline labelling is an explicit admission we lack Google's internal ranking data. This is now a published trust section on /how-it-works.
3. **Anchor the score in what Google endorses:** unique/useful/crawlable content + genuine authority (GEO-A2 already forbids artificial mentions). De-emphasized llms.txt (now informational, weight 0); schema reframed as standard SEO hygiene.

**Sales line**: "Search Console shows you appeared in Google's AI. TrustIndex AI shows you why, against whom, across every AI engine — and what to do next."

**Watch next**: Search Console AI report adds click data / expands beyond UK; any Google statement on third-party AI-citation tracking; AI Overview share climbing past 25% (raises urgency of our core pitch).
