## 1. How to run your Free AI Visibility Test

1. Go to **[ozvor.com/test](https://ozvor.com/test)**.
2. Enter your **website**, **brand**, **category**, and one **competitor**.
3. Add your **email** — your results are saved to it (and, once you create an
   account with that email, your test comes with you).
4. Click **Run**. Ozvor queries ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini and Google AI
   Overview *live* — about 60 seconds.
5. You'll see your **Ozvor AI Visibility Score**, the exact buyer prompt asked,
   and which engines cited you vs your competitor.

*If a run times out, just run it again — we show an honest gap, never a made-up
number.*

## 2. How to buy (and open) the Get-Cited Kit

1. From your free-test results or **[ozvor.com/kit](https://ozvor.com/kit)**, click
   **Get the Kit — $29**.
2. Complete secure checkout (one-time payment). You can sign in with Google /
   GitHub / LinkedIn so the Kit is saved to your account.
3. You'll get a **delivery email** from `hello@ozvor.com` with your private Kit
   link, and land on your **Kit page**.
4. On the Kit page you can **download** the Kit PDF and the companion guide, and
   read your audit, top-3 fixes, and 3 drafts.
5. Lost the link? Email **support@ozvor.com** from your purchase email and we'll
   re-send it — no second charge.

## 3. How to publish your drafts

1. Open your 3 drafts (blog post, LinkedIn post, FAQ/schema block) in the Kit.
2. Replace every `[PLACEHOLDER]` with your **real facts** — we never invent
   numbers, so those blanks are intentional.
3. Publish each where the **"where to publish" checklist** says: the FAQ/schema on
   your site, the proof post on LinkedIn, the article on your blog.
4. Give AI engines time to re-crawl, then **re-test in ~30 days** (article 4).

## 4. How to re-test in 30 days

1. AI answers move week to week, so a single audit is a snapshot.
2. Use your **30-day re-test voucher** (in the Kit) or just run the free test
   again with the same brand.
3. Compare your new score to the first one to see whether your published fixes
   moved the needle.
4. Want this automatic? **Growth** re-audits weekly and alerts you when your score
   or a competitor's position changes.

## 5. How to cancel or get a refund

- **Cancel a plan:** Account → Billing → *Manage subscription → Cancel*. It stops
  the next renewal; you keep access to the end of the current period.
- **Kit refund:** if your 3 drafts aren't ready to publish in ~10 minutes, we
  refund the $29 — we guarantee the deliverable, never AI behavior.
- **Plan refund:** Growth and Agency are covered by a **30-day money-back
  guarantee**.
- To request either, email **support@ozvor.com** from your purchase email with
  "refund" or "cancel" and we'll take care of it.
