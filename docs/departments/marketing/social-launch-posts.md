## TL;DR

The LinkedIn narrative adapted to each network's NATIVE mechanics — not
copy-paste. X gets a launch thread (no link in tweet 1; link in the last
tweet). Instagram gets a text-carousel + a Story with the link sticker (the
only clickable link IG allows). Facebook leans on the local-services / Pages
angle (that's who lives there). Threads and Bluesky get short conversational
variants — Bluesky's anti-corporate crowd is where our "our own score is
50/100" honesty lands hardest. **Reddit: DO NOT POST** (account aging, #118 —
manual participation only). No video networks this week (YouTube/TikTok): we
have no video assets and we don't fabricate. Every stat is from the verified
set already sourced in the LinkedIn doc; the evidence numbers (score 50/100,
cited 2/20) must be refreshed from /results at post time. UTM convention at
the end so GA4 attributes each network separately.
