# Tutorial: How to Benchmark Competitors
**Duration:** ~2 minutes
**Location:** /learn
**Style:** Screen recording + voiceover

## Script

**[0:00-0:10]**
"One of the most powerful features in Ozvor is the Competitor Benchmark. It shows you exactly who AI recommends instead of you — and why."

**[0:10-0:30]**
"After running your audit, the Competitors panel lists every brand AI mentioned across your prompts, ranked by citation count. You'll see each competitor's name, how many times they were cited, and on which engines."

**[0:30-0:55]**
"The 'Why they win' column is where Ozvor adds real intelligence. It analyzes their content strategy, citation sources, and authority signals. Maybe they have strong Reddit presence. Maybe their LinkedIn thought leadership is earning citations. Maybe they published a comparison page that engines trust."

**[0:55-1:20]**
"Click any competitor to drill in. You'll see which specific prompts they won, the sources citing them, and what content gaps you need to close. This turns competitive intelligence into a clear action plan."

**[1:20-1:40]**
"Use this weekly. As you publish fixes, re-run the benchmark to see if you're closing the gap. The goal isn't just a higher score — it's appearing instead of your competitors."
