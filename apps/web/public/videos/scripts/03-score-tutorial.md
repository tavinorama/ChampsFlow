# Tutorial: How to Read Your Ozvor AI Visibility Score
**Duration:** ~2 minutes
**Location:** /learn (Tutorials page)
**Style:** Screen recording + voiceover

## Script

**[0:00-0:10]**
"Your Ozvor AI Visibility Score is the single number that tells you how well AI search engines trust and cite your brand. Here's how to read it."

**[0:10-0:35]**
"The main gauge shows your overall score from 0 to 100. Above 70 means AI trusts you. Below 50 means you're invisible. Most businesses start between 40 and 60."

**[0:35-1:00]**
"Three sub-scores tell you what's driving the number. AI score measures how often engines cite you and the sentiment of those citations. Performance covers technical factors — schema markup, AI crawler access, content quality. Brand measures your off-site authority on the sources AI trusts most: Reddit, LinkedIn, Wikipedia, G2, and others."

**[1:00-1:30]**
"The trend shows your movement. Up arrows mean your fixes are working. Down arrows mean competitors are gaining ground. Every Monday, the platform recalculates so you always know if you're winning or losing."

**[1:30-1:50]**
"Use the score to prioritize. If AI is low, focus on citation-earning content. If Performance is low, fix your technical foundation. If Brand is low, invest in off-site proof."
